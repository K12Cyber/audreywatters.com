Other Projects

I did a fair amount of “Reclaim Your Domain” work this week: I deleted my Facebook account, and thanks to this great set of instructions from Doug Belshaw on ditching Gmail, am starting the process of extracting myself from Google’s tentacles. No more using Chrome. No more using Google as primary search. (Quitting Facebook is much much easier.) I’ve got a new email address now, which the infographic-spammers took no time in locating.

I also set up a PGP Key.

I collected some resources for a “learn to program” project I need to find more time for: a guide for How to Build a Twitter Bot.

A couple of thousand words written in Teaching Machines. Ugh. I’m just not as efficient as a robot-writer, am I.
