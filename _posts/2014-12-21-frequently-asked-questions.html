No.

No.

Do you do consulting?


No.

No.

No.

No.

No.

Yes, if you follow the Creative Commons license.

Probably not. And no.

Maybe. Email me and let’s talk about my availability and about what you pay.

Maybe. Email me and let’s talk about my availability and about what you pay.
