I finally walked down the street and got an official Los Angeles County Public Library card.

I bought the domain edtech.wtf. I haven’t done anything with it yet but was prompted by the new acquisition to do a little work on the Tech Gypsies site and on the Github repos associated with my annual ed-tech trends posts.

And it’s a minor thing, I realize, but count me in with the folks who are confused, if not disappointed, in the new Foursquare / Swarm app. I have used Foursquare for a really long time as it helps me remember where I’ve been and helps me find good restaurants – those where my friends have been – when I travel. It seems like it’d be pretty easy for me to ditch both apps now and just use Yelp.
